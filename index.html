Welcome to Olive Nails & Spa!


Olive Nails & Spa is a full service nail spa. We are located between Austin, Round Rock, and Pflugerville. We offer manicure, pedicure, artificial nails, waxing, facial, and eyebrow tinting. Ladies, gentlemen, and children are all welcome.

Enjoy complimentary WiFi and Beverages!

We work hard to make you - our clients - as comfortable as possible. We want you to feel as if you have just found a safe haven where you can always retreat to.
